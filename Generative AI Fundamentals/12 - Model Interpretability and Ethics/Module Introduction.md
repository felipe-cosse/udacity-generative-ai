The excitement surrounding new technologies, particularly foundation models in AI, often conjures images of a future where limitations of human capability are transcended. This sounds too good to be true. In fact, there is a critical need for vigilance. The institutions and individual actors that develop and deploy these technologies may have objectives that do not necessarily align with public interests or your interests. To become participatory citizens in the discussion of Gen AI, it's important to learn about its ethical implications.
